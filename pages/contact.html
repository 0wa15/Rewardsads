Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive my reward after redemption?

Most digital rewards (gift cards, Robux codes) are processed within 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email with your redemption code once it's processed.

Why am I not receiving points for watching ads?

Make sure you're watching the entire ad without skipping. If you're still having issues, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. If the problem persists, please contact our support team.

Can I have multiple accounts?

No, our terms of service prohibit users from creating multiple accounts. This is considered a violation and may result in account suspension.

How can I change my email address or password?

You can update your account information in your profile settings on the dashboard page. If you're having trouble accessing your account, please contact our support team.
